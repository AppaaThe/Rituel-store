## SEO tip
Shopify uses alt text for Google Image Search. These are written to include:
- Product name (RITUEL)
- Material (Amethyst, Rosehip)
- Use case (facial sculpting, lymphatic drainage, morning ritual)
- Keywords customers search for (gua sha, facial roller, ice roller)
