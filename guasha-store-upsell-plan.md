# Gua Sha Store — Hero Product + Full Upsell Stack

## The Business Model
One hero product. Every other product in your store exists to:
1. Increase the order value of someone buying the hero
2. Bring them back for a second purchase
3. Build a skincare ritual they can't complete without you

---

## Hero Product
**Gua Sha + Ice Roller Set**
- Price: $32–$38
- Positioning: "Your 5-minute morning face ritual"
- Comes in premium matte box with ritual instruction card inside

---

## Full Upsell Stack

### STEP 1 — Pre-Purchase Bundle (on the product page)

Offer bundles directly on the hero product page before they even add to cart.

| Bundle | What's Included | Price | Why They Buy It |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter Kit | Gua Sha + Ice Roller | $32 | Entry point |
| Ritual Kit ⭐ (most popular) | Gua Sha + Ice Roller + Facial Oil | $45 | Best value, most ordered |
| Full Ritual Kit | Gua Sha + Ice Roller + Facial Oil + Gua Sha Cleansing Spray | $58 | Premium option |

**Mark "Ritual Kit" as "Most Popular"** — this anchors buyers toward the middle option (most profitable).

---

### STEP 2 — Cart Page Order Bump

When they click "Add to Cart," show a one-click add-on offer:

**"Add a Facial Gua Sha Cleansing Spray — $9 (normally $14)"**
- Cleans and sanitizes the stone after each use
- Positions as essential maintenance
- One click to add, no new checkout needed
- Cost to you: $1.50–$3 | You sell for $9–$12
- Expected take rate: 25–40% of buyers

---

### STEP 3 — Checkout Page Upsell (Order Bump)

A checkbox offer shown right before they hit "Place Order":

**"☐ Add the Ritual Guidebook PDF — $7"**
- A beautifully designed digital PDF: "The 5-Minute Gua Sha Morning Ritual"
- Includes technique diagrams, pressure point maps, 30-day challenge tracker
- Cost to you: $0 (create once in Canva)
- Pure profit. 100% margin.
- Expected take rate: 20–35%

---

### STEP 4 — Post-Purchase Upsell #1 (immediately after order placed)

**Shown on the Thank You page — one-time offer, not available anywhere else.**

Use ReConvert app on Shopify.

**"Wait! Add a Jade Roller to your order — $14 (never shown at this price again)"**
- The jade roller is the companion tool to gua sha
- They already bought — no second checkout, charged to same card
- Creates a complete tool set
- Cost to you: $3–$5 | Sell for $14
- Expected take rate: 15–25%

---

### STEP 5 — Post-Purchase Upsell #2 (Thank You page, below upsell #1)

**"Complete your ritual with our Rose Hip Facial Oil — $18"**
- Gua sha works best with a facial oil as a glide (customers will Google this anyway)
- Sell them the solution before they go buy it elsewhere
- Source from Alibaba or private label from a cosmetics manufacturer
- Cost to you: $3–$6 | Sell for $18–$22
- Expected take rate: 10–20%

---

### STEP 6 — Email Sequence (post-purchase, automated)

Set up in Klaviyo. Runs automatically after every order.

| Email | Timing | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| #1 Welcome | Immediately | Brand story, what to expect, unboxing excitement |
| #2 How To Use | Day 2 | Tutorial email, builds habit and reduces returns |
| #3 Results Check-In | Day 7 | "How's your skin feeling?" — ask for review |
| #4 Refill Offer | Day 21 | "Running low on facial oil? Reorder here" |
| #5 New Product | Day 35 | Introduce your next upsell product (LED mask, steamer) |
| #6 Win-Back | Day 60 | "We miss you — here's 15% off your next order" |

---

### STEP 7 — Subscription / Refill Model

Turn one-time buyers into monthly revenue.

**"The Ritual Refill Club — $16/month"**
- Monthly delivery of: Facial Oil (30ml) + Cleansing Spray refill
- Customer subscribes once, money comes in every month
- App to use: Recharge Subscriptions (Shopify)
- Position as: "Never run out. Cancel anytime."
- Cost to you: $4–$6/month | Revenue: $16/month per subscriber
- Even 50 subscribers = $800/month recurring, hands-free

## The Simple Version to Launch With

Don't overcomplicate it. On Day 1 you only need:

1. Hero product page with 3 bundle options
2. One order bump (cleansing spray for $9)
3. One post-purchase upsell (jade roller for $14)
4. Klaviyo welcome + abandoned cart emails

That alone can take your average order from $32 → $50+ with zero extra ad spend.

Add the subscription model once you hit your first 100 orders.
