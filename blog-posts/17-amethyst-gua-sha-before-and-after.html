What kills your before and after results

  1. Skipping days. Gua sha compounds. Three sessions a week produces maybe 40% of the results of daily. This is the single biggest variable.
  2. Using too little oil. The stone drags, you leave red marks, and you stop after a week. The fix is more oil — always more.
  3. Pressing too hard. Bruising and broken capillaries actually work against you. Light pressure is the rule.
  4. Using a fake stone. Dyed glass and resin don't hold cold and don't glide smoothly. Results are dramatically worse. See our full guide on picking a real amethyst gua sha.
  5. Not doing the neck stroke. Without opening the drainage pathway, you're pushing fluid into a closed pipe. It backs up and you get zero de-puffing.

What actually amplifies your before and after

  • A pre-fridged stone. Cold is half the effect. Store it in the fridge overnight, every night.
  • Good oil underneath. Rosehip oil is our top pick — non-comedogenic, great slip, absorbs well.
  • Daily consistency. Five minutes every morning beats 20 minutes twice a week, by a wide margin.
  • Good sleep and water. Dehydrated, sleep-deprived skin doesn't drain well. Gua sha can only move what the rest of your body produces.
  • Photos in the same lighting. Take them. You won't see the changes in the mirror day-to-day, but side-by-side photos at week 1 and week 4 will shock you.
